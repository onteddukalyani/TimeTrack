📌 About TimeTrack

TimeTrack is a simple and powerful time management application designed to help students and individuals track, organize, and improve their daily productivity. Our goal is to make time tracking effortless so that users can focus on what truly matters.

🚀 Our Mission

To help people manage their time efficiently, reduce distractions, and achieve their goals with better planning and productivity.

💡 What TimeTrack Does

TimeTrack allows users to:

  • Track daily activities and tasks
  • Monitor time spent on each task
  • Set goals and deadlines
  • Analyze Productivity Patterns
  • Improve time utilization

⏱️ Time Tracking
